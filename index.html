Binding Energy dataset for CH3OH

Click on the ZPE corrected Binding Energy value to display on the Jmol applet, and start to use Jmol, the complex between water and the water cluster. To easily identified the adsorbed water, the Oxygen of that molecules is reported as Nitrogen (in blue). The Values are sorted from the lower to higher and are in kJ/mol unit.

The BE are computed at ONIOM(DLPNO-CCSD(T)/aug-cc-pvtz:xTB-GFN2)//ONIOM(B97-3c:xTB-GFN2) level. To see the zone treated with higher accuracy via ONIOM method click on "zoom".
